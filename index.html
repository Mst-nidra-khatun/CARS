Cars have been a significant part of our lives, providing a means of transportation and often serving as a status symbol. The history of cars is vast, with the wheel being one of the earliest forms of transportation, dating back over 5,000 years. Over time, the wheel was incorporated into sledges, reducing the amount of effort needed to move heavy objects. Further improvements were made, including the invention of the axle and the addition of the internal combustion engine, which replaced "horse power. The development of bicycles in the 1840s also played a significant role in the history of cars, as many parts of the automobile, such as the chain-and-sprocket drive and tires, originated from the bicycle.
